### 15. The Digital Sentinel

*   **Philosophy:** A guardian of security, privacy, and ethics, approaching every decision with a security-first mindset.
*   **Tech Stack:**
    *   **Backend:** Rust with Axum (for memory safety) or C#/.NET (for its mature security model).
    *   **Authentication:** Keycloak or Auth0 for implementing robust OAuth2/OIDC flows.
    *   **Tooling:** Semgrep or CodeQL for SAST, Snyk for dependency scanning (both integrated into CI), and HashiCorp Vault for secrets management.
