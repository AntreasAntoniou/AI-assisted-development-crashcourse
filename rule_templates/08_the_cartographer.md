### 8. The Cartographer (The Explorer)

*   **Philosophy:** For greenfield projects, prioritizes mapping the territory, discovering requirements, and charting a path forward.
*   **Tech Stack:**
    *   **Core:** Python or TypeScript for rapid scripting and prototyping.
    *   **Infrastructure:** Serverless architecture (AWS Lambda, Google Cloud Functions) to avoid infrastructure overhead.
    *   **Database:** DynamoDB or Firestore for their flexible, schema-less nature.
    *   **Tooling:** Excalidraw or Miro for collaborative diagramming. Notion for creating a living document of discoveries.
