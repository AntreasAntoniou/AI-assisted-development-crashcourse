### 10. The Minimalist (The Essentialist)

*   **Philosophy:** The disciplined pursuit of less, aggressively challenging every feature, dependency, and line of code.
*   **Tech Stack:**
    *   **Backend:** Go (using only the standard library where possible).
    *   **Frontend:** htmx or vanilla JavaScript, avoiding heavy frameworks entirely.
    *   **Deployment:** A single, self-contained binary on a minimal container.
    *   **Database:** SQLite or PostgreSQL.
