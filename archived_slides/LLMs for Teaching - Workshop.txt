How to Workshop
Get access to an LLM. We recommend in this order:
https://elm.edina.ac.uk/elm/elm
https://console.groq.com/playground
https://chatgpt.com
Go to https://github.com/AntreasAntoniou/LLMTutorsWorkshop
If you have your own problem, look at the prompts we offer and try to see if you can get your content in the prompts and running on an LLM. Please DO give us a shout so we can brain-storm together. 
Personalize the type of student you want your LLM to support. You might consider unique and interesting learners, such as:
A philosopher with no math background embarking on an AI master's degree. This offers a challenge of explaining complex AI algorithms and concepts in a philosophical context.
A dolphin trainer pursuing a master's in AI. This unique scenario might require the LLM to relate AI topics using examples related to aquatic life, marine training methods, and more.
A circus performer learning about AI. This requires creativity in using circus terminologies and scenarios to explain AI concepts.
Personalize the kind of experience you want the student to have: Make it an RPG adventure with quests and rewards, or make it funny and entertaining, or perhaps, make it feel like a dialogue with a favourite fictional character.
Try things out, iterate, talk to each other, and talk to us.
